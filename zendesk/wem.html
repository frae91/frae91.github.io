Zendesk WEM (WFM+QA)

Zendesk Workforce Engagement Management (WEM) enhances agent productivity and ensures service quality with workforce management and quality assurance tools.

Features

  • Real-time Workforce Analytics: Gain insights into agent performance, workload distribution, and customer interactions.
  • AI-driven Quality Assurance: Automatically assess support interactions, providing actionable feedback for agents.
  • Automated Scheduling: Optimize shift planning and agent allocation based on historical data and real-time demand.
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor KPIs such as adherence, occupancy, and customer satisfaction to drive continuous improvement.
