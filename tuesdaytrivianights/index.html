JOIN THE FUN

What is TUESDAY TRIVIA NIGHTS?

Tuesday Trivia Nights brings together people from their homes for a fun and healthy quiz competition. Do we do it on Tuesdays? Absolutely not! With us getting locked in our homes, who ever keeps track of the day in the week? Today is Tuesday. Tomorrow is Tuesday. Everyday is Tuesday.

How It Works

We want Tuesday Trivia Nights to be collaborative, thus every player registered for the event will have to design the questions from the category/ies that will be assigned. So each player will take turns in being the moderator, exempting the moderator from playing for that round.

At times, we also host a trivia night where we will moderate all rounds so players can just join and participate.

We hang out on Zoom for Tuesday Trivia Nights, and you can drink your poison to make things even more fun. Alcohol ban? Lol.

What Do We Get From This

If you're losing sanity from not being able to get out of your homes from the past 3-4 weeks, then this will be a perfect distraction for you. And you will meet people online who are as bored as you are.

We also wanted to make this not just a social thing, but a venue to help out in this time of crisis. How? Well, we'll be asking interested players to send off a donation through GCash (at least Php 100.00) as our "registration fee". Then we pool it together into a "pot" where the winner takes all (well technically, the winner just chooses the beneficiary where to send the prize money). Sounds Good?
